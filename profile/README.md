## What is Gumtree?

Gumtree is a marketplace app that allows users to buy and sell second-hand goods locally. With millions of active users across various categories, it’s one of the best ways to find local bargains or sell items you no longer need. From furniture and electronics to cars and even services, Gumtree provides an easy-to-use platform for both buyers and sellers.
